Mission Statement

  • To foster, enhance, and protect a unified, multi-institutional collection of retrieved orthopedic, spine, and cardiovascular implants and peri-implant tissue as a resource for academic research.
  • To pursue clinically and societally relevant studies related to implant performance through an international, collaborative, and educational exchange incorporating engineering, biology, and clinical science.
